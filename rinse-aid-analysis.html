Sheeting Technology & Chemical Analysis

Water Sheeting Science

Surface Tension Reduction

Rinse aids contain surfactants that dramatically reduce water's surface tension from 72 to 25-30 dynes/cm, allowing water to spread uniformly across surfaces rather than forming droplets that leave spots when they evaporate.

Film Formation Prevention

Advanced chelating agents bind hard water minerals (calcium, magnesium) preventing them from depositing on surfaces as white film or spots, maintaining glass clarity and dish appearance.

Accelerated Evaporation

Specialized alcohol-based compounds and evaporation enhancers speed moisture removal, reducing drying time and eliminating the need for manual towel drying in most applications.
