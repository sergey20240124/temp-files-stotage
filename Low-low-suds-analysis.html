Low-Suds Technology & Formulation Analysis

Critical Low-Suds Chemistry Requirements

Foam Control Technology

Electric glass washers with rotating brushes generate mechanical agitation that would cause excessive foam with standard detergents. Low-suds formulations use defoaming agents and specific surfactant ratios to maintain cleaning power while preventing foam buildup that would damage equipment.

Beer Head Preservation

Critical for bars and breweries - formulations must remove all soil and lipstick residues while leaving no detergent residue that would kill carbonation or reduce foam stability. This requires precise rinsing characteristics and phosphate-free chemistry.

Rapid Soil Suspension

Bar environments demand quick turnaround - detergents must immediately suspend and remove lipstick, grease, and beverage residues in 30-60 second wash cycles without pre-soaking or scrubbing.
