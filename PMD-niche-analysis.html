Commercial Formulation Technologies

High-Temperature Chemistry Requirements

Heat-Stable Surfactants

Commercial dishwashers operate at 180-195Â°F wash temperatures, requiring surfactants that maintain effectiveness and don't break down under extreme heat conditions

Alkaline Builder Systems

High-concentration sodium hydroxide and carbonate systems provide the alkalinity needed to saponify fats and neutralize organic acids at commercial volumes

Sequestering Agents

Advanced chelating compounds bind calcium and magnesium ions in hard water, preventing scale buildup and maintaining cleaning effectiveness
